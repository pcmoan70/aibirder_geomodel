# BirdNET Geomodel - Terms of Use

This document governs use of the BirdNET Geomodel software and trained model weights (the "Model"). By using the Model, you agree to these terms.

## 1. Description
BirdNET Geomodel is a spatiotemporal species occurrence prediction model that predicts which species are likely to occur at a given location and week of the year. It is designed for post-filtering BirdNET acoustic detections and general biodiversity assessment. 

Development and support involve the K. Lisa Yang Center for Conservation Bioacoustics (Cornell University), Chemnitz University of Technology and Museum für Naturkunde Berlin.
